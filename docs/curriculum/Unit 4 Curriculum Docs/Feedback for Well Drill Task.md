Feedback for Well Drill Task (End of 1st ruins)

| **Player Action** | **DANI Response** |
|----|----|
| If player drills into the 1st or 2nd layers | “Analysis: I am not observing any water. Are you certain you have drilled into the water table?” |
| If player attempts to drill into the 5th layer | “Analysis: the drill has struck bedrock and is shaking itself apart! Shutting it down now. These layers are tightly compressed, and there is no space for water. You should try drilling at a different depth.” |
| If player drill into the 3rd layer | “Analysis: I am detecting water, but it appears to be contaminated. The water may not be filtering through enough soil layers. Can you attempt to drill to a depth that would support maximum cleanliness?” |
| If player drills into the 4th layer | “Analysis: You drilled the well to the perfect depth resulting in clean water. Well done, TK.” |
