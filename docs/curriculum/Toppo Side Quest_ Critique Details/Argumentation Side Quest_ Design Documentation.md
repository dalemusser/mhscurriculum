## Rewards

Phase 1

After completing  two  arguments (combination of story and critiques)

- Toppo formalizes the player’s command by granting them the rank of Deck Cadet
- This unlocks a new uniform (a recolor of the cadet uniform) and Toppo now refers to the player as a Deck Cadet

<!-- -->

- In all cases, the player should have the option of which uniform to wear; their current rank could be noted via a badge on their arm which remains consistent no matter which uniform they have equipped
- Following military bars for this badge is likely the simplest, but where possible I would prefer to avoid implicitly militarizing students

Phase 2

After completing 5 arguments

- Toppo grants the player the rank of Third Mate
- This unlocks a new uniform (preferably slightly more elaborate than their cadet uniform) and Toppo now refers to the player as Third Mate

Phase 3

After completing 9 arguments

- Toppo grants the player the rank of Second Mate
- This unlocks a new uniform (a recolor of the Third Mate uniform) and Toppo now refers to the player as Second Mate

Phase 4

After completing 13 arguments

- Toppo grants the player the rank of First Mate
- This unlocks a new uniform (a recolor of the Third Mate uniform) and Toppo now refers to the player as First Mate

Conclusion

After completing all arguments

- Toppo grants the player the rank of Honorary Captain (which is really just a formality at this point)
- This unlocks a new uniform (preferably slightly more elaborate than the Third Mate uniform, possibly with a hat) and Toppo now consistently refers to the player as TK, without a formal title
