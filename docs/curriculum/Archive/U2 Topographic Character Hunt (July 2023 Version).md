## General Map Feedback

- If waypoint is dropped in an area that is “fogged” out on the map at any point…

	 DANI- “I don’t have map data for that area yet. You should stick to areas I have information on.”

- Players are required to place a valid waypoint (not in a fogged out region) in order to continue with dialogue for each of the three instances involved in this quest.
