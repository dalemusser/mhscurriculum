## Unit 2: Watersheds 2.0 Update

DANI

Strange, I wonder if the aliens that made this hieroglyph were studying watersheds? This looks like a picture of one. A watershed is an area of land that drains or “sheds” it’s water into a common body of water. When it rains, water flows downhill within the watershed collecting in creeks which feed into larger streams, rivers, and eventually down to the ocean. Every piece of land is part of a watershed.
