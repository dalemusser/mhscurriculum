**Underlying Facts & Principles**

- Materials that dissolve in water will move with water while the water remains in a liquid state.

- Molecules of a dissolved solid are thoroughly mixed with water molecules and cannot be separated by physical means \[cannot be separated by filtration\].

- Chemical characteristics of water \[solvent\] and the dissolved solid \[solute\] are not altered when a solution is formed.

- Substances dissolved in water will be carried by liquid water from place to place.

- Gravity and topography govern where water flows in a watershed resulting in particular connected patterns of water flow across a watershed.

  - The pattern of water movement will be determined by the topography of the watershed.

  - This governs where surface pollutants will travel

**Task Objectives:**

[**<u>Map</u>**](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1IE0zeDEIJ-jfTvvdq5W8LUFXKRxN3RneWZx_tKYGCRU/edit)

**[<u>Task 1</u>](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1EIMIpJXKbOhR_iQ1tzYb_cunoCKIBvdd-ibYTg-tdFA/edit#slide=id.g10ee87e784_2_27): <u>Supply Drop</u>**

Players search for supplies(5) that are buried in the collection area that is marked on their map. Once located, players must send five crates to base camp by floating them down the river. Players must select the correct river to successfully send the supplies to base camp.

**[<u>Task 2</u>](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1EIMIpJXKbOhR_iQ1tzYb_cunoCKIBvdd-ibYTg-tdFA/edit#slide=id.g10ee87e784_2_64): <u>Tracing Pollutant</u>**

Players are informed by Sam that there is pollution at the main base. Upon arrival, players measure pollutants at base, and tasked with finding the source of the pollution upstream from base by using pollution sensors to gather data. Once the player as identified what they believe to be the source of the pollution, they must justify their choice using the data they have collected to Jasper to obtain the expensive survey equipment.

Players must select which sites to plant their five seeds at nutrient sites. Given the location of a super nutrient(water soluble) that allows plants to grow near instantly, the player must plant five seeds in locations that will receive the super nutrient and grow rapidly.
