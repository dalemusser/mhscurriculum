# Tera Side-Quest: Creating a Catalogue

## Unit 4

*When the player returns to Tera with a picture of a desert tortoise…*

TERA

> I can’t believe you found a desert tortoise! These guys are hard to find–when it’s hot out, like, *really* hot, they hide underground to conserve water. They can even store extra water in their bladders and reabsorb it if there isn’t fresh water nearby.

PLAYER

| Seems like everyone keeps going underground in the desert. | So, they drink their own…? |
|----|----|

TERA

| Oh, I’m *sooo* gonna start calling Anderson a tortoise. You’re a genius, TK. | Basically! When it’s an animal versus the wild, anything goes to survive. |
|----|----|

*When the player returns to Tera with a picture of a kangaroo rat…*

TERA

> That’s a great pic of a kangaroo rat! They’re *real* desert survivalists–they don’t even need to drink water. I’m serious! Their bodies convert fat from the seeds they eat into water.

PLAYER

| Sounds pretty convenient! | I don’t know if I could go without water. Or soda. |
|----|----|

TERA

| Right? I’d just cram my face full of granola all the way across the desert. | With all that sugar? Ugh, be my guest. I’d rather do the tortoise thing. |
