# Tera Side-Quest: Creating a Catalogue

## Unit 5

*When the player returns to Tera with a picture of a marine iguana…*

TERA

> A marine iguana. How interesting! Did you know they can actually “sneeze” salt water out of their noses? Crazy, right? While hunting, they take in a lot of salt water. To handle this, they have special salt glands in their nasal passages. These glands help filter out excess salt from their bodies. They keep the water but get rid of the salt by snorting it out of their nose!…Gross, huh? It’s crazy what animals will do to stay hydrated!

*When the player returns to Tera with a picture of a fish…*

TERA

> Wow, a fish! They are so well adapted to living in water that most don’t even breathe like we do. In fact, most of them don’t even have lungs to take in oxygen at all. They gulp in water, which passes over special organs called gills. Those gills extract oxygen for them to use without ever having to come to the surface.
