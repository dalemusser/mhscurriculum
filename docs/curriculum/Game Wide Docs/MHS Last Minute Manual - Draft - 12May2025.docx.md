System Wide
Entering Full Screen on Chromebook
Play in full screen to prevent issues. Press Escape to free your cursor, hover to the right
edge, scroll down, and click the blue “Full Screen” icon. Do this before each unit.
Registering Player Input
Click inside the game window at the start of each unit to activate audio and register
controls.

Advancing Dialogue
Players can advance dialogue by clicking "Continue" or pressing Enter. Some scenes
may only respond to clicks.
Avoiding Settings Menu
Avoid opening the settings menu. It may disable game audio or prevent changes from
saving. Restart is required if opened.




Running
Players can run by holding Shift, though the game does not indicate this.
Using the Hoverboard
Press H to use the hoverboard after exiting the ruins in Unit 2. Using it earlier may
cause crashes.




Placeholder Notes and Visuals
Placeholders are present, especially in Unit 2 (e.g., mini-games, side quests).




Visual placeholders in Unit 3 are non-critical and can be ignored.




Unit 1
Loading Unit One
When loading Unit 1 from the Main Menu there is a small chance of a crash as shown
below.
If you encounter this crash, follow the steps below to resolve the issue:
         1.​ On the main menu, open the dev menu (ctrl + shift + 2).
         2.​ Click clear, and click save.
         3.​ Load Unit 1 from the dev menu (Click "Unit 1" button). That will reset
             everything.
         4.​ Close the dev menu (click “close” or use ctrl+shift+2)



Character Customization
Custom characters will not carry over beyond Unit 1.
Argumentation Tutorial Orb Transparency
Orbs may appear transparent or vanish during placement. This does not affect
progress. (Bug #713)




Unit Conclusion
Unit 1 concludes with a loading screen without a Space Bar to continue prompt.
Unit 2
Power Cube on Pedestal
Placing the cube may not unlock the door the first time. If DANI says it's unlocked but it
isn't, place the cube again.
Custom Waypoint Onscreen
After locating Aryn, remove the custom waypoint to avoid confusion with overlapping
markers.




Unit Conclusion
Unit 2 ends after the final argumentation scene and Anderson’s closing dialogue. No
quest prompt will appear—players should close the app at this point.




Unit 3
Missing Toppo Lesson
Before starting Unit 3, watch the Toppo Lesson here:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mzP06DKfyBM

First Sensor Drop
To drop a pollution sensor, press E while standing over water—no interaction area is
needed.
Speed of Drop
Sensors may appear not to drop due to drone and feedback speed. Advise students to
pause briefly before each drop.

Entering/Exiting the Alien Ruins
A loading screen will appear. Press Space to proceed.




Solved Particle Glyph Piece Disappearing
After solving the dissolved particle game, the final piece will disappear. It is solved, and
the player is able to progress.




Unit Conclusion
Unit 3 ends when players return to Tera after planting all four seeds and hearing
Toppo’s congratulations.
