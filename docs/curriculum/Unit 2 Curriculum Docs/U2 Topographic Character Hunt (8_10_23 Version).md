## Forced Waypoints

During the sequence where the map is opened automatically, the player must place be forced to place a waypoint and prevent progress if not placed in a valid location (I.E., not in the fog of war). 

## General Map Feedback

If waypoint is dropped in an area that is “fogged” out on the map at any point…

DANI- “I don’t have map data for that area yet. You should stick to areas I have information on.”

## U2 Time Based Feedback (“Find Toppo” Quest)

During Quest: 

If player hasn’t opened map in 2 minutes, hasn’t found Toppo, and other feedback options haven’t been triggered in 2 minutes.

DANI:  “Remember to use your map.  To open it press the “M” key. ”
