**Topographic Map Learning Progression Options**

*Objective: 2.1. Interpret topography in order to identify landmarks*

**Option \#1**:

1\. Player introduced to using the in game map (NON- curricular)

- Includes how to access the map and how to place their own waypoint only

2\. Anderson defines/ explains topography when asked how she found the player

- “Okay, this is a topographic map. You can tell because it has contour lines. Contour lines tell you where the land rises or falls. Notice where the contour lines are close together, there are very steep mountains. Where the contour lines are far apart, there is flat land…Since I knew you were in a valley, I looked at the topographic map to find a place that was flat in the middle and steep on both sides. In other words, a space where the contour lines were far apart in the middle, and were close together on the sides.”

- Anderson presents an updated Toppo [<u>Video</u>](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1RYTkm4891JMBK48Pz8vp-Mi8TRYhlUD2/view?usp=share_link) as she’s speaking to illustrate her points (or before /immediately after for the player to reference)

3\. Player practices these ideas by finding Toppo, Tera and Aryn
