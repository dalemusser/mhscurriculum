# Washington Science Learning Standards

## About

Washington was a [lead state partner in NGSS development](https://www.nextgenscience.org/lead-state-partners) and adopted the **Next Generation Science Standards** in **2013** as the **Washington State 2013 Science Learning Standards**. Standards are published by the [Washington Office of Superintendent of Public Instruction (OSPI)](https://www.k12.wa.us/).

**Code format:** Standard NGSS — `MS-ESS2-4`, etc.

## MHS Alignment

Washington uses NGSS verbatim. See **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** for the MHS alignment.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** October 4, 2013 (Washington State Board of Education / OSPI)
- **Lead state:** Yes — Washington participated in drafting NGSS.
- **Local name:** Washington State 2013 Science Learning Standards
- **Modifications:** None at the PE level. OSPI has published *ClimeTime* and other NGSS-aligned implementation supports including Washington's "Since Time Immemorial" tribal sovereignty curriculum integration.
