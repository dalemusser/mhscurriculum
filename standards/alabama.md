# Alabama Course of Study — Science

## About

Alabama's current science standards are the **Alabama Course of Study: Science**. The 2015 Course of Study has been superseded by the **2023 Alabama Course of Study: Science**, published by the [Alabama State Department of Education / Alabama Achieves](https://www.alabamaachieves.org/).

Alabama is **not formally an NGSS state**, but both the 2015 and 2023 Courses of Study **incorporate NGSS three-dimensional structure** (scientific and engineering practices, crosscutting concepts, disciplinary core ideas).

## MHS Alignment

The NGSS PE-by-PE mapping in **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** applies to Alabama with code translation. Both 2015 and 2023 Alabama standards use the three-dimensional structure that supports the same alignment claims.

## Adoption Details

- **Current standards:** 2023 Alabama Course of Study: Science (replaces 2015 version).
- **Three-dimensional:** Yes (both versions).
