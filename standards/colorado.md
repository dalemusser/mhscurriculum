# Colorado Academic Standards — Science (2020)

## About

Colorado's current science standards are the **2020 Colorado Academic Standards for Science**, published by the [Colorado Department of Education (CDE)](https://ed.cde.state.co.us/coscience). They use a three-dimensional learning approach (practices, core ideas, crosscutting concepts) and are NGSS-influenced.

**Grade-band approach:** Colorado articulates middle school science by **grade band (6–8)** rather than by individual grade for substantive content; the grade-8 CMAS assessment evaluates all middle school standards.

## MHS Alignment

Colorado's three-dimensional science standards are NGSS-aligned in substance. The MHS alignment in **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** applies with translation to Colorado's specific code format. Standards documents are downloadable per grade from CDE.

## Adoption Details

- **Current standards:** 2020 Colorado Academic Standards for Science.
- **Structure:** Three-dimensional (practices, DCIs, CCCs); grade-band articulation at middle school.
- **Assessment:** CMAS at grade 8 covers all middle school standards.
