# Wyoming Science Content and Performance Standards

## About

Wyoming's science standards are the **2016 Wyoming Science Content and Performance Standards**, approved by the Wyoming State Board of Education on **September 23, 2016** and signed into law November 14, 2016. Standards are published by the [Wyoming Department of Education](https://edu.wyoming.gov/educators/standards/science/).

Wyoming is **not formally an NGSS state** but its 2016 standards were **modeled after NGSS** with significant input from Wyoming stakeholders. The structural alignment to NGSS is strong.

**Implementation:** Required by 2020–2021 school year.

## MHS Alignment

Wyoming's standards mirror NGSS structure (three-dimensional learning, K–12 progressions). The MHS alignment in **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** applies with code translation to Wyoming's local format.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** September 23, 2016 (Wyoming State Board of Education).
- **Implementation:** Required 2020–2021.
- **NGSS-modeled:** Yes; "modernized" Wyoming standards adapt NGSS structure.
