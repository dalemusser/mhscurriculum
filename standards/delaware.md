# Delaware Science Standards

## About

Delaware was a [lead state partner in NGSS development](https://www.nextgenscience.org/lead-state-partners) and adopted the **Next Generation Science Standards** in **2013**, the same year NGSS was released. Standards are published by the [Delaware Department of Education](https://www.doe.k12.de.us/).

**Code format:** Standard NGSS — `MS-ESS2-4`, etc.

## MHS Alignment

Delaware uses NGSS verbatim. See **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** for the MHS alignment.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** September 19, 2013
- **Lead state:** Yes — Delaware participated in drafting NGSS.
- **Modifications:** None at the PE level.
