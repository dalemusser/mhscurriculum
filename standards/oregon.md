# Oregon Science Standards

## About

Oregon adopted the **Next Generation Science Standards** in **2014**, branded locally as the **Oregon Science Standards**. Standards are published by the [Oregon Department of Education](https://www.oregon.gov/ode/).

**Code format:** Standard NGSS — `MS-ESS2-4`, etc.

## MHS Alignment

Oregon uses NGSS verbatim. See **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** for the MHS alignment.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** March 6, 2014 (Oregon State Board of Education)
- **Local name:** Oregon Science Standards
- **Modifications:** None at the PE level.
