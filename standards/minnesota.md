# Minnesota Academic Standards in Science

## About

Minnesota's current science standards are the **2019 Minnesota Academic Standards in Science**, **adopted 2021** and **fully implemented 2024–2025 school year**. Standards are published by the [Minnesota Department of Education](https://education.mn.gov/MDE/dse/stds/sci/).

Minnesota was a [lead state in NGSS development](https://www.nextgenscience.org/lead-state-minnesota). The 2019 standards use the same **Framework for K-12 Science Education** as NGSS and the same **three-dimensional structure** but retain Minnesota-specific framing.

## MHS Alignment

The MN-NGSS crosswalk Minnesota publishes maps benchmarks to NGSS Performance Expectations. The MHS alignment in **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** applies through that crosswalk.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** 2021 (Minnesota Department of Education).
- **Implementation:** 2024–2025 school year (full).
- **Assessment:** Science MCA at grade 8 covers grades 6–8 benchmarks.
- **Three-dimensional:** Yes.
