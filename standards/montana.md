# Montana Science Content Standards

## About

Montana's current science standards are the **Montana Science Content Standards**, adopted by the Montana Board of Public Education in **September 2016** with an effective date of **July 1, 2017**. Standards are published by the [Montana Office of Public Instruction (OPI)](https://opi.mt.gov/Educators/Teaching-Learning/K-12-Content-Standards).

Montana was a [lead state in NGSS development](https://www.nextgenscience.org/lead-state-montana). The 2016 Montana standards are **based heavily on NGSS** and use NGSS's three-dimensional structure.

**Grade structure:** K–5 by individual grade; **6–8 and 9–12 by grade band** (matches NGSS approach for middle school).

## MHS Alignment

Montana's NGSS-based standards apply the alignment in **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** with code translation to Montana identifiers.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** September 2016 (Montana Board of Public Education).
- **Effective:** July 1, 2017.
- **NGSS-based:** Yes; Montana was an NGSS lead state.
- **Grade-band:** 6–8 (matches NGSS).
