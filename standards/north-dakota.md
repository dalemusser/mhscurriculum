# North Dakota Science Content Standards

## About

North Dakota's current science standards are the **North Dakota Science Content Standards**, adopted **February 2019** by the North Dakota Department of Public Instruction. Standards are published by [NDDPI](https://www.nd.gov/dpi/districtsschools/north-dakota-education-content-standards/science).

The 2019 standards are **based on NGSS** and use the three-dimensional structure (Science and Engineering Practices, Disciplinary Core Ideas, Crosscutting Concepts).

## MHS Alignment

The MHS alignment in **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** applies to North Dakota with code translation.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** February 2019 (NDDPI).
- **NGSS-based:** Yes.
