# Idaho Content Standards — Science

## About

Idaho's science standards are the **Idaho Content Standards for Science**, NGSS-aligned with three-dimensional structure (Science and Engineering Practices, Crosscutting Concepts, Disciplinary Core Ideas). Standards are published by the [Idaho State Department of Education](https://www.sde.idaho.gov/about-us/departments/content-and-curriculum/science/).

Idaho is **not formally an NGSS state**. The Idaho Content Standards for Science were developed using the *Framework for K-12 Science Education* (NRC, 2012) as a basis.

## MHS Alignment

Idaho's three-dimensional structure mirrors NGSS. The MHS alignment in **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** applies with code translation to Idaho-specific identifiers.

## Adoption Details

- **Three-dimensional:** Yes.
- **Middle school standards** are organized by topic (Life Science, Earth/Space Science, Physical Science).
- **Grade range:** 6, 7, 8.
