# West Virginia College and Career Readiness Standards for Science

## About

West Virginia's current science standards are the **West Virginia College and Career Readiness Standards for Science**, **built on the Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS) and the Framework for K-12 Science Education**. Standards are administered by the [West Virginia Department of Education](https://wvde.us/middle-secondary-learning/science/standards-and-guidance/) under WVBE policy.

West Virginia is **not formally an NGSS state** — the standards use NGSS as a basis but are published as state-branded "College and Career Readiness Standards."

## MHS Alignment

Because WVCCR-S is built on NGSS, the alignment in **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** applies with code translation. The West Virginia Science Teachers Association publishes grade-by-grade Science Standard Supports for grades 6, 7, and 8.

## Adoption Details

- **Local name:** West Virginia College and Career Readiness Standards for Science.
- **NGSS-based:** Yes — built on NGSS and the K-12 Framework.
