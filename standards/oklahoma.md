# Oklahoma Academic Standards (OAS) for Science

## About

Oklahoma's current science standards are the **2020 Oklahoma Academic Standards (OAS) for Science**, published February 2020 by the [Oklahoma State Department of Education](https://sde.ok.gov/science). They were **first assessed in the 2022–2023 school year**.

OAS uses **three-dimensional performance expectations** (the same structural pattern as NGSS) — Science and Engineering Practices, Disciplinary Core Ideas, and Crosscutting Concepts integrated into each PE. Oklahoma is **not formally an NGSS state** but the structural alignment is direct.

**Grade structure:** Standards are by individual grade for K–8; Grade 6 (p. 51), Grade 7 (p. 64), Grade 8 (p. 78) in the 2020 OAS document.

## MHS Alignment

OAS three-dimensional PEs map closely to NGSS PEs. The MHS alignment in **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** applies with code translation to Oklahoma identifiers.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** February 2020.
- **First assessed:** 2022–2023 school year.
- **Three-dimensional:** Yes.
