# Illinois Learning Standards for Science

## About

Illinois adopted the **Next Generation Science Standards** in **2014**, branded locally as the **Illinois Learning Standards for Science**. Standards are published by the [Illinois State Board of Education (ISBE)](https://www.isbe.net/).

**Code format:** Standard NGSS — `MS-ESS2-4`, etc.

## MHS Alignment

Illinois uses NGSS verbatim. See **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** for the MHS alignment.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** February 2014 (ISBE)
- **Local name:** Illinois Learning Standards for Science
- **Modifications:** None at the PE level.
