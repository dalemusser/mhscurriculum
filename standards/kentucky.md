# Kentucky Academic Standards for Science

## About

Kentucky was a [lead state partner in NGSS development](https://www.nextgenscience.org/lead-state-partners) and adopted the **Next Generation Science Standards** in **2013**, branded locally as the **Kentucky Academic Standards (KAS) for Science**. Standards are published by the [Kentucky Department of Education](https://education.ky.gov/).

**Code format:** Standard NGSS — `MS-ESS2-4`, etc.

## MHS Alignment

Kentucky uses NGSS verbatim. See **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** for the MHS alignment.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** June 5, 2013 (Kentucky Board of Education)
- **Lead state:** Yes — Kentucky participated in drafting NGSS.
- **Local name:** Kentucky Academic Standards (KAS) for Science
- **Modifications:** None at the PE level.
