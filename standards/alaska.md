# K-12 Science Standards for Alaska

## About

Alaska's current science standards are the **K-12 Science Standards for Alaska**, **NGSS-based** with Alaskan-specific clarifications. Standards are published by the [Alaska Department of Education and Early Development](https://education.alaska.gov/standards/science).

Alaska adopted NGSS as the basis for its science standards because of the three-dimensional structure and the focus on science for all students. The standards add Alaska-specific clarification statements (e.g., connections to Alaskan phenomena like glaciers, permafrost, fisheries) but the underlying Performance Expectations are NGSS.

**Code format:** NGSS PE codes (`MS-ESS2-4`, etc.) with Alaska-specific Clarification Statements and Assessment Boundary Statements.

## MHS Alignment

Alaska uses NGSS PEs verbatim. The MHS alignment in **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** applies directly. The Alaska-specific Clarification Statements add local-context examples but do not change PE substance.

## Adoption Details

- **Current standards:** K-12 Science Standards for Alaska (NGSS-based).
- **Local additions:** Clarification Statements with Alaska context; Assessment Boundary Statements.
