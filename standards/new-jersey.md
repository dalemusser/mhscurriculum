# New Jersey Student Learning Standards — Science

## About

New Jersey adopted the **Next Generation Science Standards** in **2014**, branded locally as the **New Jersey Student Learning Standards for Science (NJSLS-S)**. Standards are published by the [New Jersey Department of Education](https://www.nj.gov/education/).

**Code format:** Standard NGSS — `MS-ESS2-4`, etc.

## MHS Alignment

New Jersey uses NGSS verbatim. See **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** for the MHS alignment.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** July 9, 2014 (New Jersey State Board of Education)
- **Local name:** New Jersey Student Learning Standards for Science (NJSLS-S)
- **Modifications:** None at the PE level. NJ has published its own implementation guidance and "Climate Change Across the Content Areas" overlay (2020), which integrates climate-related expectations across subjects without modifying the underlying NGSS PEs.
