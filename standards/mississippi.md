# Mississippi College- and Career-Readiness Standards (MS CCRS) for Science

## About

Mississippi's current science standards are the **2018 Mississippi College- and Career-Readiness Standards for Science (MS CCRS)**, replacing the 2010 Mississippi Science Framework. Standards are published by the [Mississippi Department of Education](https://mdek12.org/academiceducation/mississipp-college-and-career-readiness-standards/).

MS CCRS is structured around three content strands: **Life Science, Physical Science, Earth and Space Science**. The standards reflect national expectations while remaining Mississippi-specific.

Mississippi is **not formally an NGSS state**.

## MHS Alignment

MS CCRS Earth and Space Science standards in grades 6–8 cover the water cycle, watersheds, and human impact at varying depth. Direct PE-by-PE alignment to NGSS requires consultation of the official 2018 MS CCRS document; conceptual alignment to MHS Units 2–6 exists.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** 2018 (replaces 2010 Mississippi Science Framework).
- **Three content strands:** Life, Physical, Earth and Space.
