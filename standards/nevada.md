# Nevada Academic Content Standards in Science

## About

Nevada adopted the **Next Generation Science Standards** in **2014**, branded locally as the **Nevada Academic Content Standards in Science**. Standards are published by the [Nevada Department of Education](https://doe.nv.gov/).

**Code format:** Standard NGSS — `MS-ESS2-4`, etc.

## MHS Alignment

Nevada uses NGSS verbatim. See **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** for the MHS alignment.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** August 8, 2014 (Nevada State Board of Education)
- **Local name:** Nevada Academic Content Standards in Science
- **Modifications:** None at the PE level.
