# Louisiana Student Standards for Science (LSS)

## About

Louisiana's current science standards are the **Louisiana Student Standards for Science (LSS)**, adopted **2017**. Standards are published by the [Louisiana Department of Education](https://doe.louisiana.gov/school-system-leaders/academic-strategy/louisiana-student-standards).

LSS is **based on the Framework for K-12 Science Education** (NRC, 2012) — the same foundation as NGSS — and uses the **three-dimensional structure** (Science and Engineering Practices, Disciplinary Core Ideas, Crosscutting Concepts).

## MHS Alignment

LSS and NGSS share the same foundational framework. The MHS alignment in **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** applies with code translation. Louisiana publishes its own LSS-NGSS crosswalk for grade 6.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** 2017 (Louisiana Department of Education).
- **Three-dimensional:** Yes.
