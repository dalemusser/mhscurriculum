# Rhode Island Science Standards

## About

Rhode Island was a [lead state partner in NGSS development](https://www.nextgenscience.org/lead-state-partners) and adopted the **Next Generation Science Standards** in **2013**. Standards are published by the [Rhode Island Department of Education (RIDE)](https://ride.ri.gov/).

**Code format:** Standard NGSS — `MS-ESS2-4`, etc.

## MHS Alignment

Rhode Island uses NGSS verbatim. See **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** for the MHS alignment.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** November 21, 2013 (Rhode Island Council on Elementary and Secondary Education)
- **Lead state:** Yes — Rhode Island participated in drafting NGSS.
- **Modifications:** None at the PE level.
