# District of Columbia Science Standards

## About

The District of Columbia adopted the **Next Generation Science Standards** in **2014**. Standards are administered by the [DC Office of the State Superintendent of Education (OSSE)](https://osse.dc.gov/).

**Code format:** Standard NGSS — `MS-ESS2-4`, etc.

## MHS Alignment

DC uses NGSS verbatim. See **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** for the MHS alignment.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** December 2014 (DC State Board of Education)
- **Local name:** DC Science Standards (verbatim NGSS)
- **Modifications:** None at the PE level.
