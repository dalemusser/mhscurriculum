# New Mexico Science Standards

## About

New Mexico adopted the **Next Generation Science Standards** in **2017**, after a public review process. Standards are published by the [New Mexico Public Education Department](https://www.ped.state.nm.us/).

**Code format:** Standard NGSS — `MS-ESS2-4`, etc.

## MHS Alignment

New Mexico uses NGSS verbatim plus New-Mexico-specific additions (see below). See **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** for the MHS alignment of the core PEs.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** September 19, 2017 (New Mexico Public Education Department)
- **Local name:** New Mexico STEM-Ready! Science Standards
- **Modifications:** New Mexico **adopted the full NGSS** plus a small set of state-specific additions emphasizing local context (e.g., New Mexico geography and culture). The MHS-relevant PEs are unchanged.
- **Notable adoption history:** Initial 2017 draft attempted to omit references to evolution, climate change, and the age of Earth; following public outcry, NMPED restored the standards to full NGSS form before final adoption.
