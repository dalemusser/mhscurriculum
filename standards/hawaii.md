# Hawaii Science Standards

## About

Hawaii adopted the **Next Generation Science Standards** in **2016** as the Hawaii State Department of Education's official science standards. Standards are published by the [Hawaii State Department of Education](https://www.hawaiipublicschools.org/).

**Code format:** Standard NGSS — `MS-ESS2-4`, etc.

## MHS Alignment

Hawaii uses NGSS verbatim. See **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** for the MHS alignment.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** 2016 (Hawaii State Board of Education)
- **Modifications:** None at the PE level.
