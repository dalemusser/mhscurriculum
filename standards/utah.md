# Utah Science with Engineering Education (SEEd) Standards

## About

Utah's science standards are the **Science with Engineering Education (SEEd) Standards**, adopted by the Utah State Board of Education in **2015** and implemented in grades 6–8 starting the **2017–2018 school year**. Standards are published by the [Utah State Board of Education](https://schools.utah.gov/curr/science).

SEEd uses NGSS's **three-dimensional structure** (Science and Engineering Practices + Crosscutting Concepts + Disciplinary Core Ideas) explicitly. Each standard integrates at least one SEP, one CCC, and one DCI. Utah does not formally adopt NGSS but the structural and substantive alignment is strong.

## MHS Alignment

The NGSS PE-by-PE mapping in **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** applies to Utah SEEd with code translation. Three-dimensional integration is identical in structure.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** 2015 (Utah State Board of Education).
- **Implementation:** 2017–2018 school year for grades 6–8.
- **Three-dimensional:** Yes — explicit SEP, CCC, DCI per standard.
