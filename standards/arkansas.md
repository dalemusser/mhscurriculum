# Arkansas Science Standards

## About

Arkansas adopted the **Next Generation Science Standards** for grades K–8 in **2015**, with high school adoption following. The standards are published by the [Arkansas Department of Education](https://dese.ade.arkansas.gov/) as the "Arkansas K–12 Science Standards."

**Code format:** Standard NGSS — `MS-ESS2-4`, `MS-ESS3-1`, etc.

## MHS Alignment

Arkansas uses NGSS verbatim for grade-band 6–8 science. The Performance Expectations directly cited by MHS, the adjacent ones, and the SEP-7 argumentation thread are all documented in **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** — that file is the alignment reference for Arkansas.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** 2015 (K–8); high school subsequently
- **Local name:** Arkansas K–12 Science Standards
- **Modifications from NGSS:** None substantive at the PE level; standard NGSS three-dimensional structure preserved.
