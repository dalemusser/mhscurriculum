# South Carolina College- and Career-Ready Science Standards 2021

## About

South Carolina's current science standards are the **South Carolina College- and Career-Ready Science Standards 2021**, approved by the South Carolina State Board of Education and the Education Oversight Committee on **June 14, 2021**. Standards are published by the [South Carolina Department of Education](https://ed.sc.gov/instruction/standards/science/).

South Carolina is **not formally an NGSS state**, but the 2021 standards are **explicitly three-dimensional Performance Expectations** with NGSS-style Science and Engineering Practices, Disciplinary Core Ideas, and Crosscutting Concepts. The structural alignment to NGSS is very strong; the substantive content closely tracks NGSS PEs.

**Structure:** SC 2021 PEs combine an SEP, one or more DCIs, and a CCC in the NGSS three-dimensional pattern. Code format is SC-specific but PE content is NGSS-equivalent.

## MHS Alignment

Because SC 2021 mirrors NGSS three-dimensional structure, the alignment in **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** applies to South Carolina with code translation. The five directly cited PEs, five adjacent PEs, and SEP-7 argumentation thread all map.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** June 14, 2021 (SC State Board of Education + Education Oversight Committee).
- **Local name:** South Carolina College- and Career-Ready Science Standards 2021.
- **Three-dimensional structure:** Yes — SEP/DCI/CCC explicitly listed for each PE.
- **Prior version:** South Carolina Academic Standards and Performance Indicators for Science 2014.
