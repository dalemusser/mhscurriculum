# New Hampshire College and Career Ready Science Standards

## About

New Hampshire adopted the **Next Generation Science Standards** in **2016**, branded locally as the **New Hampshire College and Career Ready Science Standards**. Standards are published by the [New Hampshire Department of Education](https://www.education.nh.gov/).

**Code format:** Standard NGSS — `MS-ESS2-4`, etc.

## MHS Alignment

New Hampshire uses NGSS verbatim. See **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** for the MHS alignment.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** 2016 (New Hampshire State Board of Education)
- **Local name:** New Hampshire College and Career Ready Science Standards
- **Modifications:** None at the PE level.
