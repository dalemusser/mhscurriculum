# South Dakota Science Standards

## About

South Dakota's current science standards are the **South Dakota Science Standards**. The state has adopted multiple revisions; the most recent revision was **April 22, 2024**. South Dakota was a [lead state in NGSS development](https://www.nextgenscience.org/lead-state-south-dakota). Standards are published by the [South Dakota Department of Education](https://doe.sd.gov/contentstandards/science.aspx).

South Dakota's standards are described as a **near-copy of NGSS** — three-dimensional structure, the same numbering system, and substantively identical wording for most standards. A small number of NGSS PEs were modified or omitted (notably MS-ESS1-4 on geologic time scale).

## MHS Alignment

The MHS alignment in **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** applies essentially verbatim to South Dakota — the PE codes, dimensions, and content are NGSS-equivalent for the MHS-relevant standards (none of the modified/omitted ones touch MHS content).

## Adoption Details

- **Most recent adoption:** April 22, 2024.
- **Lead state in NGSS:** Yes.
- **Near-copy of NGSS:** Same three-dimensional structure, numbering, and wording for nearly all standards.
