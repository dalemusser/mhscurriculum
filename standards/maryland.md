# Maryland Science Standards

## About

Maryland was a [lead state partner in NGSS development](https://www.nextgenscience.org/lead-state-partners) and adopted the **Next Generation Science Standards** in **2013**. Standards are published by the [Maryland State Department of Education (MSDE)](https://www.marylandpublicschools.org/).

**Code format:** Standard NGSS — `MS-ESS2-4`, etc.

## MHS Alignment

Maryland uses NGSS verbatim. See **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** for the MHS alignment.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** June 25, 2013 (Maryland State Board of Education)
- **Lead state:** Yes — Maryland participated in drafting NGSS.
- **Modifications:** None at the PE level.
