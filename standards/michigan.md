# Michigan K–12 Science Standards

## About

Michigan adopted the **Next Generation Science Standards** in **2015**, branded locally as the **Michigan K–12 Science Standards**. Standards are published by the [Michigan Department of Education](https://www.michigan.gov/mde).

**Code format:** Standard NGSS — `MS-ESS2-4`, etc.

## MHS Alignment

Michigan uses NGSS verbatim. See **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** for the MHS alignment.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** November 10, 2015 (Michigan State Board of Education)
- **Local name:** Michigan K–12 Science Standards
- **Modifications:** None at the PE level.
