# Iowa Science Standards

## About

Iowa adopted the **Next Generation Science Standards** in **2015** as the Iowa Core Science Standards. Standards are published by the [Iowa Department of Education](https://educate.iowa.gov/).

**Code format:** Standard NGSS — `MS-ESS2-4`, etc.

## MHS Alignment

Iowa uses NGSS verbatim. See **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** for the MHS alignment.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** August 2015 (Iowa State Board of Education)
- **Local name:** Iowa Core Science Standards
- **Modifications:** None at the PE level.
