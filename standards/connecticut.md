# Connecticut Science Standards

## About

Connecticut adopted the **Next Generation Science Standards** in **2015**, with full implementation in 2018–2019. Standards are published by the [Connecticut State Department of Education](https://portal.ct.gov/SDE).

**Code format:** Standard NGSS — `MS-ESS2-4`, etc.

## MHS Alignment

Connecticut uses NGSS verbatim. See **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** for the MHS alignment.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** November 4, 2015
- **Implementation:** Statewide by 2018–2019; assessed via the NGSS-aligned Next Generation Science Assessment
- **Local name:** Connecticut adopted NGSS as published; no separate state name applied.
- **Modifications:** None at the PE level.
