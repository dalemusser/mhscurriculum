# Maine Science Standards

## About

Maine adopted the **Next Generation Science Standards** in **2018** as part of the Maine Learning Results revision. Standards are published by the [Maine Department of Education](https://www.maine.gov/doe/).

**Code format:** Standard NGSS — `MS-ESS2-4`, etc.

## MHS Alignment

Maine uses NGSS verbatim. See **[ngss.md](ngss.md)** for the MHS alignment.

## Adoption Details

- **Adopted:** 2018 (Maine State Board of Education)
- **Local name:** Maine Learning Results — Science
- **Modifications:** None at the PE level.
